# <Docs.Name of="Switch" />

A variation of <Docs of="Checkbox" /> that has a more explict-feeling "off"
state. Whereas a group of co-located <Docs.Name of="Checkbox" /> feels more like
multiple options for one field, a group of <Docs.Name of="Switch" /> feels more
like discrete fields under a common category.
